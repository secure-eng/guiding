Our Story & Mission

Guiding Hands Placement Services was founded by Catherine Fitzgerald and Kathleen Goodwin, with over 30 years of experience in healthcare. They started the service to help families navigate complex care decisions with clarity and confidence.

Our Mission

To support seniors and families with clear, compassionate guidance when making important care decisions.

Our Vision

To be a trusted care advisory service known for integrity, professionalism and personalized support.

Our Values

Compassion, honesty, respect and a commitment to putting people first in everything we do.
