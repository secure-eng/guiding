Our Senior Care Services

At Guiding Hands Placement Services we provide a full range of senior care solutions designed to ensure comfort safety dignity and emotional wellbeing for every senior.

Assisted Living

Assisted living communities are designed for seniors who value independence but need support with daily activities such as bathing dressing medication management meals and mobility. These communities provide private apartments social engagement dining services and 24 hour staff support offering a balance of independence safety and community. Guiding Hands Senior Placement Services helps families determine when assisted living may be the right next step. We assess your loved one’s care needs explain pricing and levels of support and connect you with trusted communities that fit your budget and preferences. We guide you through tours comparisons and decision making while providing clarity advocacy and peace of mind throughout the process.

Home Care

Home care is professional support provided in the comfort of a person’s home to assist with daily activities personal care companionship and sometimes skilled medical needs. It allows seniors to maintain independence and remain in familiar surroundings while receiving the assistance necessary to stay safe and supported. Guiding Hands Senior Placement Services helps families understand their home care options by assessing individual needs explaining levels of care and connecting them with trusted reputable providers. We simplify the process advocate for your loved one and provide ongoing guidance so you can feel confident knowing the right support is in place.

Hospice

Hospice care is specialized support for individuals facing a life limiting illness focused on comfort dignity and quality of life rather than curative treatment. Hospice provides pain management symptom control emotional support spiritual support and guidance for both the patient and their family. Care can be provided in the home assisted living community or hospice facility wherever the individual feels most comfortable. Guiding Hands Senior Placement Services helps families understand when hospice may be appropriate explains what services are covered and connects them with compassionate reputable providers. We walk beside you during this emotional time offering clarity advocacy and steady support so your loved one can receive care centered on comfort and peace.

Memory Care

Memory care is specialized support designed for individuals living with Alzheimer’s disease dementia or other cognitive impairments. These communities provide a secure structured environment with trained staff who understand the unique challenges of memory loss. Care focuses on safety routine cognitive engagement and assistance with daily living while promoting dignity and quality of life. Guiding Hands Senior Placement Services helps families determine when memory care may be the safest and most supportive option. We assess your loved one’s needs explain care levels and costs and connect you with trusted memory care communities that align with your family’s values and budget. We provide guidance and reassurance every step of the way helping you make informed decisions with confidence and compassion.

Medicaid & Financial Services

Navigating Medicaid and long term care costs can feel overwhelming for families already facing emotional decisions. Understanding eligibility requirements asset guidelines and application processes is often confusing and time sensitive. Guiding Hands Senior Placement Services helps families make sense of Medicaid and financial assistance options by explaining qualification criteria outlining required documentation and connecting you with trusted professionals who specialize in Medicaid applications and long term care planning. We help you understand what may be covered what costs to anticipate and how to protect your loved one’s resources whenever possible. Our goal is to reduce stress prevent costly mistakes and ensure your family has a clear financial path forward so you can focus on your loved one’s care and not paperwork or uncertainty.
