Our Senior Care Services

At Guiding Hands Placement Services, we provide a full range of senior care solutions designed to ensure comfort, safety, dignity, and emotional wellbeing for every senior.

Assisted Living

Helping families find safe, comfortable, and engaging assisted living communities tailored to their needs.

Home Care

Providing in-home support services that promote independence, safety, and personalized care for seniors.

Hospice

Offering compassionate end-of-life care focused on comfort, dignity, and emotional support for both seniors and families.

Memory Care

Specialized programs and environments designed to support individuals with dementia or Alzheimer's safely and respectfully.

Medicaid & Financial Services

Assisting families in navigating Medicaid, insurance, and financial planning to simplify access to senior care resources.

Case Management

Coordinating medical care, support services, and community resources to ensure a smooth, safe, and quality care experience.
